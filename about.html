About Ivy Origin

Independent, practical, and built around what small businesses actually need.

Founded by an independent web developer focused on building practical websites for small businesses.

Ivy Origin works with clinics, coaching institutes, and other local businesses that need a website which actually does its job — bring in inquiries, look trustworthy, and stay simple to manage.

The focus stays on small businesses, with a working style built on direct client collaboration: you're talked to directly through the whole build, from the first call to launch.

I focus on clarity and usability over complexity. Every site is customized to what the client actually wants, not forced into a fixed template.
